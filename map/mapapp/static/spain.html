MONEY HEIST

Money Heist follows a brilliant mastermind and his team as they carry out daring, high-pressure robberies filled with strategy, suspense, and emotion.

Money Heist is a Spanish crime-thriller series centered on The Professor, who plans an audacious heist at the Royal Mint of Spain to print billions of euros. The robbers, wearing red jumpsuits and Salvador Dalí masks, become symbols of rebellion. Characters like Tokyo, Berlin, Nairobi, Rio, Denver, and others contribute their unique skills. The series is known for its intense suspense, emotional depth, and clever storytelling.
